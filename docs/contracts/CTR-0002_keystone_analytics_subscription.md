# SUBSCRIPTION SERVICES AGREEMENT

**Contract ID (internal):** CTR-0002
**Contract Name:** Keystone Analytics — Annual Subscription

## 1. Parties

This Subscription Services Agreement ("Agreement") is between **Nimbus Retail Co.** ("Customer") and **Keystone Analytics Labs, LLC** ("Vendor").

## 2. Term

**Effective Date:** July 1, 2025
**Initial Term:** Twelve (12) months, ending **June 30, 2026**.

## 3. Renewal

This Agreement **automatically renews** for successive twelve (12) month terms unless written notice of non-renewal is delivered by either Party at least **thirty (30) days** prior to the end of the then-current term.

## 4. Fees and Payment Terms

- **Annual Subscription Fee:** USD 420,000, invoiced quarterly in advance.
- **Contract Value Cap:** USD 420,000 per annual term. Overages require written change order.
- **Payment Terms:** **Net 30** days from invoice date.
- **Currency:** USD.

## 5. Scope

Vendor provides its cloud-based analytics platform, including (a) up to 200 named users, (b) unlimited dashboards, (c) 10 TB query volume per month, and (d) standard integrations.

## 6. Service Level

Vendor commits to 99.9% monthly uptime. Planned maintenance windows shall be communicated at least seventy-two (72) hours in advance and do not count toward downtime.

## 7. Termination

Termination for cause: thirty (30) days to cure a material breach. Termination for convenience is **not permitted** during the Initial Term. During Renewal Terms, either Party may terminate for convenience on ninety (90) days notice.

## 8. Data Protection

Vendor shall process Customer data in accordance with the Data Processing Addendum attached as Exhibit A and shall not share Customer data with third parties except as required to deliver the Services.

## 9. Governing Law

Commonwealth of Massachusetts.
