# LEGAL SERVICES RETAINER AGREEMENT

**Contract ID (internal):** CTR-0004
**Contract Name:** Harbor Legal — Retainer Agreement

## 1. Parties

This Retainer Agreement is between **Nimbus Retail Co.** ("Client") and **Harbor Legal Advisors LLP** ("Firm"), a firm registered in England and Wales.

## 2. Term

**Effective Date:** 1 April 2025
**Initial Term:** Twelve (12) months, ending **31 March 2026**.

## 3. Renewal

This Agreement shall **not automatically renew**. Any continuation beyond the Initial Term requires a new signed engagement letter. Non-renewal notice period is **sixty (60) days**.

## 4. Fees and Payment Terms

- **Monthly Retainer:** USD 15,000 covering up to 40 partner/associate hours per month.
- **Contract Value Cap:** USD 180,000 across the Initial Term, exclusive of pass-through expenses.
- **Payment Terms:** **Net 30** days from invoice date.
- **Currency:** USD (Firm will invoice in USD notwithstanding UK domicile).

## 5. Scope of Services

General corporate counsel, commercial contracts review, employment advisory, and UK/EU regulatory monitoring. Litigation, M&A, and IP prosecution are out of scope and require a separate engagement letter.

## 6. Conflicts and Confidentiality

Firm shall comply with the Solicitors Regulation Authority Code of Conduct and shall not accept representations materially adverse to Client during the term.

## 7. Termination

Either Party may terminate this Agreement upon thirty (30) days written notice. Fees for work-in-progress are payable through the termination date.

## 8. Governing Law

England and Wales.
