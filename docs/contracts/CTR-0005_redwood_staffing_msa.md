# MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT — STAFFING AUGMENTATION

**Contract ID (internal):** CTR-0005
**Contract Name:** Redwood Staffing — MSA + SOW 2026

## 1. Parties

This Agreement is between **Nimbus Retail Co.** ("Customer") and **Redwood Staffing Group, Inc.** ("Supplier").

## 2. Term

**Effective Date:** January 15, 2026
**Initial Term:** Twelve (12) months, ending **January 14, 2027**.

## 3. Renewal

This Agreement **automatically renews** for successive twelve (12) month terms unless written non-renewal notice is delivered at least **thirty (30) days** prior to the end of the current term.

## 4. Fees and Payment Terms

- **Contract Value Cap:** USD 650,000 across the Initial Term.
- **Billing Model:** Hourly rate card per Exhibit A; monthly invoicing.
- **Payment Terms:** **Net 45** days from invoice date.
- **Currency:** USD.

## 5. Scope

Supplier provides contract-to-hire and project-based staffing in Engineering, Data, and Product functions. Customer is not obligated to minimum monthly volume.

## 6. Compliance and Background

Supplier shall conduct background checks, verify work authorization, and maintain E-Verify enrollment for all placed personnel.

## 7. Conversion Fees

Customer may convert placed personnel to employee status; conversion fees decrease on a sliding scale:
- Within 0-90 days: 25% of annualized base
- 91-180 days: 15%
- 181-365 days: 0%

## 8. Termination

Either Party may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon ninety (90) days written notice, or for material breach upon thirty (30) days written notice and failure to cure.

## 9. Risk and Insurance

Supplier shall maintain workers' compensation, commercial general liability (USD 2M), and professional liability (USD 1M) coverage.

## 10. Governing Law

State of California.
