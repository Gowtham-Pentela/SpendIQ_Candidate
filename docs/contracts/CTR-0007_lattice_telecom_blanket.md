# BLANKET PURCHASE AGREEMENT

**Contract ID (internal):** CTR-0007
**Contract Name:** Lattice Telecom — Network Services Blanket Agreement

## 1. Parties

This Blanket Purchase Agreement is between **Nimbus Retail Co.** ("Customer") and **Lattice Telecom Networks, Inc.** ("Provider").

## 2. Term

**Effective Date:** April 1, 2024
**Initial Term:** Twenty-seven (27) months, ending **June 30, 2026**.

## 3. Renewal

This Agreement shall **automatically renew** for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either Party delivers written notice of non-renewal at least **ninety (90) days** prior to the end of the then-current term.

## 4. Fees and Payment Terms

- **Aggregate Spend Cap:** USD 540,000 across the Initial Term.
- **Rate Card:** As specified in Exhibit A; includes MPLS circuits, mobile fleet lines, and toll-free services.
- **Payment Terms:** **Net 30** days from invoice date.
- **Currency:** USD.

## 5. Scope

Provider will deliver network services on a call-off basis against the rate card in Exhibit A. Provider is a non-exclusive supplier.

## 6. Service Level Agreement

- **Circuit Uptime:** 99.99% for MPLS lanes; 99.5% for secondary access.
- **Incident Response:** 30 minutes for Severity 1; 4 hours for Severity 2.
- **Credits:** Specified in Exhibit B, capped at one month of recurring charges per lane per month.

## 7. Change Control

Material changes to the rate card, including tariff pass-throughs exceeding 3%, require prior written Customer approval.

## 8. Termination

Either Party may terminate for material breach upon thirty (30) days written notice and failure to cure. Customer may terminate for convenience upon sixty (60) days notice with cancellation fees equal to fifty percent (50%) of remaining committed charges.

## 9. Governing Law

State of Texas.
