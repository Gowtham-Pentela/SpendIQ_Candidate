# BLANKET PURCHASE AGREEMENT — OFFICE SUPPLIES

**Contract ID (internal):** CTR-0008
**Contract Name:** Meridian Office Supply — Blanket PO

## 1. Parties

This Agreement is between **Nimbus Retail Co.** ("Buyer") and **Meridian Office Supply Co.** ("Supplier").

## 2. Term

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2025
**End Date:** **December 31, 2026**

## 3. Renewal

This Agreement shall **automatically renew** for successive twelve (12) month periods unless either Party delivers written notice of non-renewal at least **thirty (30) days** prior to the end of the current term.

## 4. Fees and Payment Terms

- **Annual Spend Cap:** USD 95,000.
- **Catalog Pricing:** Discounted off published list; refresh quarterly.
- **Payment Terms:** **Net 45** days from invoice date.
- **Currency:** USD.

## 5. Scope

Office supplies, breakroom consumables, print materials, and basic ergonomic accessories against the online catalog.

## 6. Service Level

- **Order Fulfillment:** 48 business hours for stocked items.
- **Order Accuracy:** 99%.
- **Damage Returns:** Free returns within 30 days.

## 7. Termination

Either Party may terminate this Agreement for convenience upon thirty (30) days written notice, or for material breach upon fifteen (15) days written notice and failure to cure.

## 8. Governing Law

State of Ohio.
