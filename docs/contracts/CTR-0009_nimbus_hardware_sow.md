# STATEMENT OF WORK — IT DEVICE REFRESH

**Contract ID (internal):** CTR-0009
**Contract Name:** Nimbus IT Hardware — Device Refresh SOW

## 1. Parties

This Statement of Work is entered between **Nimbus Retail Co.** ("Buyer") and **Nimbus IT Hardware Inc.** ("Supplier"), a company registered in Ontario, Canada.

> Note: The Supplier's name "Nimbus IT Hardware Inc." is not affiliated with the Buyer ("Nimbus Retail Co."); the naming collision is coincidental and is acknowledged by both Parties.

## 2. Term

**Effective Date:** August 1, 2025
**End Date:** **July 31, 2026**

## 3. Renewal

This SOW shall **not automatically renew**. Continuation requires execution of a new SOW. Notice window for expiration notifications: **sixty (60) days**.

## 4. Fees and Payment Terms

- **SOW Value Cap:** USD 880,000.
- **Line Items:** 800 laptops, 400 monitors, 200 docks, peripherals; pricing per Exhibit A.
- **Payment Terms:** **Net 30** days from invoice date.
- **Currency:** USD (Supplier to invoice in USD).

## 5. Deliverables and Schedule

- Wave 1 delivery: October 2025 (300 laptops, 150 monitors).
- Wave 2 delivery: February 2026 (300 laptops, 150 monitors).
- Wave 3 delivery: May 2026 (200 laptops, 100 monitors, peripherals).
- Asset tagging, imaging, and drop-ship to designated Nimbus sites.

## 6. Warranty

- Laptops and monitors: 3-year manufacturer warranty with next-business-day onsite support.
- RMA processing: 5 business days.

## 7. Termination

Either Party may terminate this SOW for material breach upon thirty (30) days written notice and failure to cure. Buyer may cancel un-shipped waves upon thirty (30) days notice subject to a 10% cancellation fee on affected line items.

## 8. Governing Law

State of Washington (USA). Both Parties agree to U.S. jurisdiction notwithstanding Supplier's Canadian domicile.
