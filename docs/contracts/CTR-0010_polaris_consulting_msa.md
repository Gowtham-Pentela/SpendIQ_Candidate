# MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT — CONSULTING

**Contract ID (internal):** CTR-0010
**Contract Name:** Polaris Consulting — Transformation MSA

## 1. Parties

This Master Services Agreement is between **Nimbus Retail Co.** ("Client") and **Polaris Consulting Group, LLC** ("Consultant").

## 2. Term

**Effective Date:** September 1, 2024
**Initial Term:** Twenty-four (24) months, ending **August 31, 2026**.

## 3. Renewal

This Agreement shall **automatically renew** for successive twelve (12) month terms unless a Party delivers written non-renewal notice at least **sixty (60) days** prior to the end of the then-current term.

## 4. Fees and Payment Terms

- **Aggregate Value Cap:** USD 1,250,000 across the Initial Term.
- **Work is performed under SOWs** executed under this MSA; each SOW specifies deliverables, fees, and timelines.
- **Payment Terms:** **Net 45** days from invoice date.
- **Currency:** USD.
- **Travel and Expenses:** Reimbursed per Client's Travel Policy (see policies/expense_payment_policy.md).

## 5. Scope Areas

Operating model design, process transformation, data strategy, M&A integration support. Specific engagements are governed by SOWs.

## 6. Independence and Conflicts

Consultant represents that it has no engagement that would constitute a material conflict of interest with Client's transformation program. Consultant shall disclose potential conflicts prior to accepting an SOW.

## 7. Intellectual Property

Client retains ownership of Client Confidential Information and Client-specific work product. Consultant retains its pre-existing methodologies and tools.

## 8. Termination

Either Party may terminate the MSA or any SOW for convenience upon thirty (30) days written notice. Fees for services rendered and non-cancellable commitments are payable through termination.

## 9. Governing Law

State of Illinois.
