# FACILITIES SERVICES SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT

**Contract ID (internal):** CTR-0011
**Contract Name:** Juniper Facilities — Cleaning Services

## 1. Parties

This Agreement is between **Nimbus Retail Co.** ("Customer") and **Juniper Facilities Services, LLC** ("Provider").

## 2. Term

**Effective Date:** July 1, 2024
**Initial Term:** Twenty-four (24) months, ending **June 30, 2026**.

## 3. Renewal

This Agreement shall **automatically renew** for successive twelve (12) month terms unless a Party delivers written non-renewal notice at least **thirty (30) days** prior to the end of the current term.

## 4. Fees and Payment Terms

- **Contract Value Cap:** USD 210,000 across the Initial Term.
- **Service Model:** Monthly flat fee of USD 8,750 covering scope in Section 5.
- **Payment Terms:** **Net 30** days from invoice date.
- **Currency:** USD.

## 5. Scope

Nightly janitorial services at Customer's corporate HQ and three (3) distribution centers identified in Exhibit A. Periodic deep-cleaning, restroom supplies, and waste removal included.

## 6. Service Level

- **Quality Inspections:** Monthly Customer walkthrough; target score 90%.
- **Incident Response:** Same-day response for safety concerns.
- **Labor Standards:** Provider shall comply with local wage and employment laws and provide workers' compensation coverage.

## 7. Termination

Either Party may terminate this Agreement for material breach upon fifteen (15) days written notice and failure to cure. For convenience termination, sixty (60) days written notice is required.

## 8. Governing Law

State of Florida.
