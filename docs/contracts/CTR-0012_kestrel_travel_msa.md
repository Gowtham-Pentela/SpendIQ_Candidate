# CORPORATE TRAVEL SERVICES AGREEMENT

**Contract ID (internal):** CTR-0012
**Contract Name:** Kestrel Travel — Corporate Travel Agreement

## 1. Parties

This Agreement is between **Nimbus Retail Co.** ("Customer") and **Kestrel Travel Partners, Inc.** ("TMC").

## 2. Term

**Effective Date:** February 1, 2025
**Initial Term:** Twenty-four (24) months, ending **January 31, 2027**.

## 3. Renewal

This Agreement shall **not automatically renew**. Continuation requires a mutually executed amendment. Notice of intent to negotiate renewal: **ninety (90) days** prior to term end.

## 4. Fees and Payment Terms

- **Managed Travel Cap:** USD 450,000 in TMC fees across the Initial Term. Airfare, hotel, and ground transportation pass-throughs are excluded from the cap.
- **Transaction Fees:** USD 18 per online booking; USD 32 per agent-assisted booking.
- **Payment Terms:** **Net 30** days from invoice date.
- **Currency:** USD.

## 5. Scope

- Corporate travel booking platform with duty-of-care integration.
- 24x7 agent support.
- Quarterly business reviews and savings reports.
- Negotiated supplier program management (air, hotel, car).

## 6. Service Level

- **Platform Uptime:** 99.7%.
- **Agent Answer Time:** 90% of calls within 30 seconds.
- **Reconciliation Accuracy:** 99% on monthly statements.

## 7. Data Privacy

TMC shall process traveler personal data in compliance with applicable privacy laws and shall execute a Data Processing Addendum with Customer.

## 8. Termination

Either Party may terminate for convenience upon ninety (90) days written notice, or for material breach upon thirty (30) days notice and failure to cure.

## 9. Governing Law

State of Georgia.
