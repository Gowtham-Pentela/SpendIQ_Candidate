# SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT — DR & BACKUP SERVICES

**Contract ID (internal):** CTR-0014
**Contract Name:** Summit Cloud — DR & Backup Services

## 1. Parties

This Agreement is between **Nimbus Retail Co.** ("Customer") and **Summit Cloud Networks, Inc.** ("Provider").

## 2. Term

**Effective Date:** May 1, 2025
**Initial Term:** Twelve (12) months, ending **April 30, 2026**.

## 3. Renewal

This Agreement shall **automatically renew** for successive twelve (12) month terms unless written non-renewal notice is delivered at least **thirty (30) days** prior to the end of the current term.

> **Operational note:** Given the 30-day notice window, non-renewal must be decided by the end of the month preceding renewal.

## 4. Fees and Payment Terms

- **Annual Fee Cap:** USD 240,000 per annual term.
- **Payment Terms:** **Net 30** days from invoice date.
- **Currency:** USD.

## 5. Scope

- Cross-region data replication for Customer's production systems.
- Daily encrypted snapshots with 90-day retention.
- Annual DR tabletop exercise and one (1) failover test.
- Incident support for recovery events.

## 6. Service Level

- **Replication Lag:** < 15 minutes for Tier-1 systems.
- **RPO / RTO:** 15 min / 4 hours for Tier-1 systems.
- **Support Availability:** 24x7 for Severity 1 and 2 events.

## 7. Termination

Either Party may terminate this Agreement for material breach upon thirty (30) days written notice and failure to cure. Termination for convenience is available during Renewal Terms only, on sixty (60) days notice.

## 8. Governing Law

State of Colorado.
