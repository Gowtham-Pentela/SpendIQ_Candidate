# PERFORMANCE MARKETING RETAINER AGREEMENT

**Contract ID (internal):** CTR-0015
**Contract Name:** Beacon Marketing — Performance Retainer

## 1. Parties

This Agreement is between **Nimbus Retail Co.** ("Client") and **Beacon Marketing Partners, Inc.** ("Agency").

## 2. Term

**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**End Date:** December 31, 2026

## 3. Renewal

This Agreement shall **not automatically renew**. Renewal requires a new executed Agreement. Notice of intent to renew or non-renew: **sixty (60) days** prior to term end.

## 4. Fees and Payment Terms

- **Monthly Retainer:** USD 30,000.
- **Contract Value Cap:** USD 360,000 across the Initial Term.
- **Performance Bonus Pool:** Up to USD 75,000 tied to KPIs in Section 6; billed quarterly in arrears against achievement.
- **Payment Terms:** **Net 30** days from invoice date.
- **Currency:** USD.

## 5. Services

Paid search, paid social, and marketplace optimization. Account management, creative production, and reporting included.

## 6. Performance KPIs

- **Blended CPA:** below USD 45.
- **ROAS:** 4.2x or higher.
- **Incremental Revenue:** USD 12M attributable lift over calendar 2026.

## 7. Data and Tool Access

Agency shall access Client advertising accounts under Client-owned credentials and shall not reuse Client-specific learnings or audiences for other clients.

## 8. Termination

Either Party may terminate for convenience upon sixty (60) days written notice. Termination for material breach: thirty (30) days notice and failure to cure. Upon termination, Client retains all creative work delivered and paid for.

## 9. Governing Law

State of New York.
